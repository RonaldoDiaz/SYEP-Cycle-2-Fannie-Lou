Get up and Stay Active

Exercise is an activity requiring physical effort, carried out especially to sustain or improve health and fitness. Basketball makes you fit in many ways by burning calories, cardiovascular health, helps you develop concentration and self discipline, build bone strength, better motor skills and coordination, strength training, improved spatial awareness, mental development, reduces stress and boosts the immune system, and it enhances confidence.

Another activity that can make you fit is by playing Football. You can get a better work ethic, stress relief, an overall workout, mental health, teamwork, cardio, interval training, cardiovascular health, and increased bone density and mass.

Baseball can also keep you fit because of cardiovascular training, you get strong arms and legs, and you can burn calories and you have a better hand-eye coordination, burn calories, stress relief, and an overall fitness.
