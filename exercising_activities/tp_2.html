What is fat?

Fat is a oily/greasy substance, its deposited as a layer under the skin or around some certain organs.

Fat is also good for you because it gives you energy and it helps your body abord some nutrients and produces important hormones that your body needs to help you in your daily life.

BMI stands for body mass index. BMI is measured from body fat based on height and weight,the higher the BMI the higher probability of being obese.
